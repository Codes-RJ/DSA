# Interview Revision Strategy

## Revision Layers

1. revise theory terms
2. revise common patterns
3. re-solve previously missed problems
4. practice explaining complexity aloud

## Weekly Revision Rule

- one day for old problems
- one day for theory refresh
- one mixed timed session

## What to Track

- topics still weak
- mistakes repeated more than once
- templates you rely on often
- patterns you fail to identify quickly
